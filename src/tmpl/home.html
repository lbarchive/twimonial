  • What is Twimonial?
  • How to Add a Testimonial?
Twimonial is a webapp to read testimonials about Twitter users. You can also make a list for your Follow Friday recommendation so that your followers get to know why a user that you recommend should be followed. For Example: 
#FF @postsecret @cookingupastory @claraskitchen @ImprovEvery Read my #twimonial at http://j.mp/5Ikq95

It's very simple. You do not need to register here or authorize this website to access. You only need to tweet. There are two prerequisites to form a testimonial:

  1. You and the testimonial receiver, both accounts need to be public.
  2. You must follow that Twitter user.

If you are going to write a testimonial about @screen_name, you will tweet in either way of the following sample tweets:

  • Broadcasting
  • @reply
This guy is smart, intelligent, active and always replies. The tweets are mostly about geeky stuff, Linux, etc. #twimonial @screen-name

Place #twimonial and @screen-name at the end of tweet, you can not change the order. All of your followers can read your testimonial on their Twitter home page. 

@screen-name This guy is smart, intelligent, active and always replies. The tweets are mostly about geeky stuff, Linux, etc. #twimonial

Use @reply and put #twimonial at the end of tweet, you testimonial will not show on your followers' Twitter home page.

A few facts you might want to know:

  • You can only write a testimonial about a Twitter user.
  • You can update testimonial, just tweet with new testimonial.
  • The pages usually have minutes long caching time. Please be patient.
