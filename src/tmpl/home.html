What is Twimonial?

Twimonial is a webapp to read testimonials about Twitter users. You can also make a list for your Follow Friday recommendation so that your followers get to know why a user that you recommend should be followed. For Example: 
#FF @postsecret @cookingupastory @claraskitchen @ImprovEvery Read my #twimonial at http://j.mp/5Ikq95

How to Add a Twimonial?

It's very simple. You do not need to register here or authorize this website to access. You only need to tweet. There are four prerequisites to form a Twimonial:

  1. Your Twitter account and the Twitter account you are about to write a Twimonial for, both need to be Public accounts.
  2. You must follow that Twitter account.
  3. You must use @reply.
  4. You must put the hashtag #twimonial at the end of the tweet.
If you are going to write a Twimonial about @screen_name, you will tweet: 
@screen-name This guy is smart, intelligent, active and always replies. The tweets are mostly about geeky stuff, Linux, etc.#twimonial
A few facts you might want to know:
  • You can only write a Twimonial for a Twitter user, the content is limited to lesser than 140 characters.
  • You can update, you just tweet again with new Twimonial.
  • The pages usually have minutes long caching time. Please be patient.
