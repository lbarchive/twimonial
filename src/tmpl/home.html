If you are going to write a testimonial about @name, you will tweet in either way of the following sample {{ config.TWEET_NAME|lower }}s:

  • Broadcasting
  • @reply

Place {{ config.TRACKING_HASHTAG}} and @name at the end of {{ config.TWEET_NAME|lower }}, you can not change the order. All of your {{ config.FOLLOWERS_NAME|lower }} can read your testimonial on their {{ config.SERVICE_NAME }} home page.

Use @reply and put {{ config.TRACKING_HASHTAG }} at the end of {{ config.TWEET_NAME|lower }}, you testimonial will not show on your {{ config.FOLLOWERS_NAME|lower }}' {{ config.SERVICE_NAME }} home page.
